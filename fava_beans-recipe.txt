Fava beans (foul)

1. Skillet on high, add olive oil
2. Add chopped onions (half)
3. add 3 cubes of garlic
4. Let cook a little bit (2 min~)
5. Add chopped tomatoes (2 medium ones)
6. Bring heat to low and add the beans
7. Quickly mash them as fast as possible
8. Add spices and cover it
9. Wait till you see it bubble and cook with spices for a bit
